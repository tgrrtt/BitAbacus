Info

Portfolio, help, and contact information.

What is BitAbacus?

BitAbacus is a webservice that keeps track of your cryptocurrency holdings. As more and more cryptocurrencies are introduced, and as the price fluctuates wildly, it can be hard to tell exactly what your cryptocurrency net-worth is at any given moment. BitAbacus aims to take all the work out of this by automatically retrieving your cryptocurrency info from exchanges and wallets and showing you the conversions to bitcoin and fiat.

How?

BitAbacus uses info APIs to get information on your holdings on various exchanges. This means your info will be kept current.

Is this secure?

In order for the APIs to be secure, one must use info APIs. Supported exchanges allow the user to set the permissions of the API. As long as the APIs in use on BitAbacus are set to "info only" on their respective exchanges, no harm can be done.
